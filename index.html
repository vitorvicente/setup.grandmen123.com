Introduction

This setup is the culmination of over 5 years of purchases and improvements to mount a gaming rig that not only allows me to play, record and stream games in high definition, but also work on extensive projects in the most efficient way possible.

Since I currently live abroad, I've had to make some adjustments, which led me to effectively have two different setups, all the components are listed bellow.
